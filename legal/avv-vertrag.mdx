    ## 1. Gegenstand und Dauer der Verarbeitung

    Der Auftragsverarbeiter erbringt für den Verantwortlichen technische Dienste im Rahmen des Produkts Obserra Bot. Hierbei werden personenbezogene Daten von Discord-Nutzern verarbeitet, um die Funktionen des Bots (z. B. Moderation, Tickets, Logging, Statistiken, Dashboard) bereitzustellen.

    Die Dauer der Verarbeitung entspricht der Laufzeit des zugrundeliegenden Nutzungsvertrags (AGB). Nach Vertragsende werden Daten gemäß Abschnitt 9 gelöscht.

    ## 2. Art und Zweck der Verarbeitung

    Die Verarbeitung erfolgt ausschließlich zur:

    - Bereitstellung und Verwaltung des Discord-Bots,
    - Durchführung von Support- und Verwaltungsfunktionen,
    - Analyse von Server-Aktivitäten (z. B. Moderationslogs, Tickets),
    - Synchronisation und Datensicherung.

    Andere Zwecke sind ausgeschlossen, es sei denn, eine gesetzliche Pflicht besteht.

    ## 3. Art der verarbeiteten Daten

    Verarbeitet werden u. a. folgende personenbezogene Daten:

    - Discord-User-IDs, Benutzernamen, nutzerprofile, avatare, nutzeraktivitäten, Server-IDs
    - Nachrichteninhalte (soweit zur Funktion erforderlich)
    - Rollen, Kanalzuordnungen, Tickets, Moderationsdaten, server einstellungen
    - technische Nutzungsdaten (z. B. Zeitstempel, Log-Einträge, ip-adressen)

    ## 4. Kategorien betroffener Personen

    Mitglieder und Administratoren des Discord-Servers

    Nutzer und Aministratoren die unsere Webservices u.a. das Dashboard ([dashboard.obserra.net](http://dashboard.obserra.net)) nutzen

    ## 5. Rechte und Pflichten des Verantwortlichen

    Der Verantwortliche bleibt im datenschutzrechtlichen Sinne Verantwortlicher gemäß Art. 4 Nr. 7 DSGVO.

    Er ist für die Rechtmäßigkeit der Erhebung, Verarbeitung und Nutzung personenbezogener Daten verantwortlich.

    Er hat das Recht, sich vor Beginn und während der Verarbeitung von der Einhaltung der technischen und organisatorischen Maßnahmen (TOMs) durch den Auftragsverarbeiter zu überzeugen.

    **1. Subject Matter and Duration of Processing**

    The Processor provides technical services to the Controller within the scope of the Obserra Bot product. In doing so, personal data of Discord users are processed to provide the bot's functions (e.g., moderation, tickets, logging, statistics, dashboard).

    The duration of the processing corresponds to the term of the underlying user agreement (General Terms and Conditions - GTC). After the end of the contract, data will be deleted according to Section 9.

    **2. Nature and Purpose of Processing**

    Processing is carried out exclusively for:

    - Providing and managing the Discord bot,
    - Performing support and administrative functions,
    - Analyzing server activities (e.g., moderation logs, tickets),
    - Synchronization and data backup.

    **3. Type of Data Processed**

    The following personal data are processed, among others:

    - Discord user IDs, usernames, user profiles, avatars, user activities, server IDs
    - Message content (as far as necessary for the function)
    - Roles, channel assignments, tickets, moderation data, server settings
    - Technical usage data (e.g., timestamps, log entries, IP addresses)

    Members and administrators of the Discord server\
    Users and administrators who use our web services, including the dashboard ([dashboard.obserra.net](http://dashboard.obserra.net))

    **5. Rights and Obligations of the Controller**

    The Controller remains the Controller in the sense of data protection law pursuant to Art. 4 No. 7 GDPR.

    The Controller is responsible for the lawfulness of the collection, processing, and use of personal data.

    The Controller has the right to verify the Processor's compliance with technical and organizational measures (TOMs) before and during processing.

    **6. Obligations of the Processor**

    The Processor processes data exclusively on documented instructions from the Controller.

    Persons involved in processing at the Processor are obliged to maintain confidentiality.

    The Processor shall implement the necessary technical and organizational measures in accordance with Art. 32 GDPR (see Section 7).

    The Processor supports the Controller in fulfilling the rights of data subjects (Art. 12–23 GDPR).

    The Processor shall immediately inform the Controller of data breaches (Art. 33 GDPR).

    **7. Technical and Organizational Measures (TOMs)**

    The Processor has implemented the following measures:

    - Access protection through authentication and rights management
    - Transport encryption (TLS/HTTPS)
    - Protection against unauthorized access (Firewall, IP control)
    - Regular backups and monitoring
    - Logging of security-relevant events
    - Pseudonymized storage where possible
    - Internal data protection policies and access controls

    A detailed list of measures can be provided upon request.

    **8. Sub-processing Relationships**

    The Processor may only engage sub-processors if they demonstrably implement suitable data protection measures and a corresponding DPA exists. Currently engaged sub-processors are (as of 11/2025):

    - Discord Inc., 444 De Haro St, San Francisco CA 94107 (USA) — Platform infrastructure
    - Hetzner Online GmbH, Gunzenhausen, Germany — Hosting and server systems
    - Potentially further service providers according to the current data protection information at [**https://docs.obserra.net/legal/privacy**](https://docs.obserra.net/legal/privacy)

    **9. Deletion and Return of Data**

    Upon termination of the contractual relationship, the Processor shall delete all personal data after the expiry of statutory retention periods. The Controller may request a data copy before the end of the contract, insofar as technically possible.

    **10. Proof Obligations and Audits**

    The Processor shall provide the Controller with all necessary information upon request to demonstrate compliance with the GDPR. Audits can be conducted electronically or in writing in consultation.

    **11. Liability**

    Liability is governed by statutory provisions and by the agreements in the GTC of Obserra Bots.

    **12. Final Provisions**

    Amendments or additions to this contract require text form.

    Should any provision be ineffective, the validity of the remaining provisions shall remain unaffected.

    German law applies.
